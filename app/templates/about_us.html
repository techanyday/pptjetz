About Us

PPTJet was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to eliminate the friction of presentation creation. We understand how time-consuming it can be to design, format, and structure slide decks from scratch. Whether you're a student racing a deadline, a busy professional managing internal updates, or a startup founder prepping a pitch, PPTJet helps you turn ideas into clean, structured presentations in seconds.

Our team combines the talents of educators, designers, and software engineers who believe that great ideas shouldn’t be held back by design skills. With AI at the core, PPTJet helps you skip the formatting and get straight to the point—making your workflow faster, simpler, and more productive.

User-Centric Simplicity

We’ve interviewed hundreds of users—from university students to SaaS startup founders—and discovered they value speed, clarity, and structure above all else. Every product decision we make, from the layout themes to the export formats, is guided by those needs.

Automation Without Limitation

While we offer a curated set of four high-quality templates—Aesthetic, Vintage, Creative, and Simple—our real strength lies in the AI-generated content structure behind them. You’re not locked into a static template; you’re starting with a smart foundation you can customise and evolve after export. Focus on your message, not the mechanics.

Our Vision

As we grow, we’re committed to delivering even more intuitive tools that empower creators of all kinds—from educators and marketers to solo founders and enterprise teams. PPTJet is more than a tool—it’s a smarter way to communicate ideas.
