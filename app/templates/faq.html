Frequently Asked Questions

Is PPTJet really free?

Yes! Our Free plan allows you to generate up to 3 presentations per month.

Can I customize the slides?

Absolutely. You can export the presentation and edit it in Microsoft PowerPoint or Google Slides.

Do I need to install anything?

No downloads or installations are required. PPTJet runs entirely in your browser.

Are my ideas or data stored?

We do not store your presentation content. Your data remains private.

How many slide themes are available?

Currently, we offer 4 professionally designed themes.

Will more features be added soon?

Yes. We're working on image charts, brand kits, and analytics tools.

Does it work on mobile?

PPTJet is optimised for desktop but also functions on mobile devices. You can generate presentations from your phone and view or edit them as long as you have a mobile PowerPoint-compatible app installed.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@pptjet.com or use the live chat on our site.
